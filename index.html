Game Rules

Welcome to SaaS Simulator! Your goal is to maximize revenue and customer growth over a 3-year period.

  • Draw cards to simulate business events.
  • Make strategic decisions about pricing, marketing, technology, and partnerships.
  • Manage your costs, churn rate, and customer acquisition.
  • The game ends after 36 turns (3 years).
  • The winner is the company with the highest total revenue and customer base.
